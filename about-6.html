About This Practice

Eboni Morris is a licensed independent insurance agent appointed with multiple recognized insurance carriers. Licensed since 2017 in Life, Accident, and Sickness, she works with individuals across a broad spectrum of health profiles - from those in excellent health to those managing serious or complex medical conditions - to identify coverage options that may fit their situation, subject to individual carrier underwriting guidelines and approval.

Our Standards

The commitments that
govern every engagement.

I.

Carrier Evaluation

Available products are evaluated across appointed carriers based on each client's individual profile and coverage objectives.

II.

HIPAA-Compliant Privacy

All personal and health information is handled in strict compliance with federal HIPAA privacy law. No information is shared with any third party without the client's explicit written authorization.

III.

Pre-Application Underwriting

We assess each carrier's underwriting guidelines against the client's profile before any formal application is submitted - preserving the client's medical record and mitigating unnecessary declinations.

IV.

No-Pressure Advisory

This practice operates as an advisor - not a sales organization. Clients are presented with options and make their own decisions. No application is submitted without explicit instruction and consent.

V.

Broad Condition Coverage

Our carrier relationships include companies that underwrite coverage for clients with complex medical histories, multiple medications, and high-risk conditions not accepted by standard carriers.

VI.

Informed Decision-Making

Clients are provided with a complete explanation of all recommended products - structure, cost, duration, and long-term obligations - prior to any application being initiated.

Who We Serve

From excellent health
to complex conditions.

Every individual deserves access to information about their coverage options. This practice works with clients across the full health spectrum - not exclusively those in perfect health. The carriers we are appointed with include companies that evaluate a wide range of health profiles, and coverage options are explored based on each client's individual circumstances at the time of application.

Excellent Health

Clients in good to excellent health may qualify for standard rate classifications and full immediate benefit coverage across a broad range of carriers and face amounts.

Managed Conditions

Clients managing conditions such as diabetes, high blood pressure, or depression may qualify for standard or modified coverage depending on current treatment status, medications, and carrier guidelines.

Complex or High-Risk Profiles

Clients with significant health histories, prior declines, or conditions such as cancer history, heart disease, HIV, or organ transplant may find coverage options through carriers that specialize in higher-risk underwriting, subject to their individual guidelines.

Coverage availability is not guaranteed and is subject to individual carrier underwriting guidelines at the time of application. Nothing on this page constitutes a guarantee or offer of coverage.
